About Me

Hello, I am Ekaterina Furman. Since my teenage years I have always dabbled with HTML, CSS, and PhotoShop, but never took the chance to make it my career ... until about 2 years ago, when I suddenly became a single mother.

At that time I was recommended to pursue a career in Cyber Security, so studied for a year getting my CompTIA A+, NET+, and SEC+ certifications. However, that path did not feel right for me.

Which is when I discovered The Odin Project. I never thought you could be self-taught in the web development industry, but The Odin Project gave me that confidence to start.

From there on I got a few Udemy courses from Jonas Schmedtmann, among some others, and have been dedicating myself to become a Front End Developer.
