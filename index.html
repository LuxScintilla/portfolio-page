About Me

Hello, I am Ekaterina Furman. Through life I have always dabbled with HTML, CSS, and PhotoShop, as a hobby. About 2 years ago I suddenly became a single mother.

At that time I was recommended to pursue a career in Cyber Security, so studied for a year getting my CompTIA A+, NET+, and SEC+ certifications. However that path did not feel right.

Which is when I discovered The Odin Project. I never thought you could be self-taught in the web development industry, but The Odin Project gave me that confidence to start. From there on I got a few courses from Udemy from Jonas Schmedtmann and have been dedicating all the free time left to my study, to become a Front End Developer.
